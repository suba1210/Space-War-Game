SPACE WAR




INSTRUCTIONS FOR THE WAR

  • You are able to move the Space-Ship all four directions.
    • Arrow Up : Move the space-ship Up
    • Arrow Down : Move the space-ship Down
    • Arrow Right : Move the space-ship Right
    • Arrow Left : Move the space-ship Left
    • Space Bar: Release Bullet from space-ship
  • You can also use the buttons given in the Game Area for moving the Space-Ship
  • For the first 100 seconds your SpaceShip will be in Mission1
  • For the next 100 seconds your SpaceShip will be advanced to Mission2
  • In Mission 1 , You will gain 3 points for each Shoot of the enemy
  • In Mission 2, You will gain only one point for each Shoot of the enemy
  • You will gain extra 30 points, when you manage to catch each powerUps.
